New York Citibike Analysis
A Tableau Dashboard of Public Ride Data from the New York Citibike Ride-Sharing Program.

Two years of public ride-sharing data (2017 and 2018) was obtained from the New York Citibike ride-sharing program, the largest bicycle ride-sharing program in the United States. The data was aggregated and cleaned for use in a Tableau Public Dashboard. In the available data, it appears that most riders are male yearly-pass holders and peak use occurs during rush hour, suggesting the bicycles are often used for commutes.

The dashboard may be viewed here.

Tools:

  • Pandas (Python)
